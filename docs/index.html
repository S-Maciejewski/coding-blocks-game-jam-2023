Reverse Battleships

Your goal is to place all of your ships first, then when the bombs start dropping try to move your ships to avoid getting hit

Click and drag the ships into position. Once they are placed you'll be able to move them to green tiles (their available moves)

Bombs drop with a timer and explode once the timer (in turns) expires

You can't move over destroyed ships and bombs that exploded recently

Controls

R - restart game

Click and drag - move ships

Right click (while moving ship) - rotate ship
